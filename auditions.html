Auditions

Come Out to Auditions !

We're excited to be holding virtual auditions for Spring 2021 on Jan 28th (Thursday) and Jan 29th (Friday)! To audition, please sign up for a slot here. Also, make sure to fill out the information form here or scan the QR code on the flyer below.

Prepare up to a 1 minute solo of any song (in any language) that showcases your musical capabilities. Please send an audio recording of yourself singing your audition song (title the recording with your full name) to ucberkeleydilse@gmail.com before your audition date.

For more information, check out our event page. We look forward to meeting you all!
